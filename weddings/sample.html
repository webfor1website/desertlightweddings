Our Love Story

We met on a sunny afternoon at a coffee shop in downtown Phoenix. Sarah was studying for her nursing boards, and Michael was working on his laptop. When their eyes met across the room, something clicked.

After months of coffee dates, hiking adventures in the Arizona desert, and countless sunsets at Camelback Mountain, Michael proposed at sunset at the same spot where they first met. Sarah said yes before he even finished asking.

Now we're excited to start our next chapter together, surrounded by our favorite people in the beautiful Arizona desert we both call home.
