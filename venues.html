Scottsdale Wedding Venues We Love

150 weddings across Arizona means we know these venues like the back of our hand. The light at El Chorro at golden hour. The gardens at Four Seasons Scottsdale. The rooftop views at The Scott. Every venue has its magic — we know where to find it.

El Chorro Lodge Scottsdale

The historic Adobe building and desert landscape create incredible backlight opportunities. Golden hour here is unmatched — the light filters through the old trees and hits the ceremony space in ways you can't plan for. Indoor spaces have warm, rustic tones that pair perfectly with natural light.

Four Seasons Resort Scottsdale

Manicured gardens and mountain views provide endless backdrop options. The outdoor terraces offer soft, even light throughout the day. Indoor ballrooms have large windows that let in plenty of natural light for reception details and getting-ready shots.

The Scott Resort and Spa

Rooftop ceremonies with panoramic views of Camelback Mountain. The pool area creates beautiful reflections during golden hour. The modern architecture offers clean lines and geometric patterns that work beautifully for wide shots and detail shots alike.

Andaz Scottsdale Resort

Contemporary desert design with incredible indoor-outdoor flow. The courtyard spaces offer consistent light throughout the ceremony. The bold colors and modern aesthetic create striking portraits that feel fresh and editorial.

Sanctuary Camelback Mountain

Mountain views and desert luxury in equal measure. The elevation provides unique angles and dramatic lighting as the sun sets. The spa and infinity pool areas offer serene, intimate settings for portraits away from the crowd.

The Saguaro Scottsdale

Vibrant colors and mid-century modern architecture make for bold, energetic photos. The bright pink exterior creates incredible contrast against blue desert skies. Indoor spaces are equally colorful and offer great options for reception details.

Hyatt Regency Scottsdale

Water features and desert landscaping provide variety throughout the property. The outdoor ceremony spaces have consistent shade which is crucial for Arizona weddings. The resort layout offers multiple locations for different types of shots without traveling far.

The Phoenician

Mountain views and luxury amenities create a polished backdrop. The golf course offers wide-open spaces for dramatic portraits. The terraces and balconies provide elevation for unique angles on the ceremony and reception.

Silverleaf Club

Private desert community with stunning mountain backdrops. The natural desert landscape provides texture and depth to portraits. The clubhouse offers elegant indoor spaces with large windows for getting-ready and detail shots.

Gainey Ranch Golf Club

Green fairways against desert mountains create classic wedding photo compositions. The clubhouse architecture offers both indoor and outdoor options. Sunset light here is particularly dramatic with long shadows across the course.

Villa Siena Phoenix

European-inspired architecture with incredible indoor options. The grand staircase and ballroom provide elegant backdrops for formal portraits. The courtyard offers a beautiful outdoor ceremony space with consistent light.

The Paseo

Old Town Scottsdale charm with modern amenities. The outdoor spaces have great shade coverage for ceremonies. The surrounding desert landscape provides natural framing for portraits without leaving the property.

Desert Botanical Garden Phoenix

Desert flora and mountain views in every direction. The paths and trails provide natural leading lines for portraits. Golden hour here is spectacular with the desert plants silhouetted against the setting sun.
